Top Pick: Delilah by Maison Alhambra for
Women

Romantic. Feminine. Radiant.

Delilah is a graceful and luminous fragrance that celebrates femininity in its most elegant
form. Inspired by Delina, it opens with a bright and juicy blend of lychee, rhubarb, and
bergamot, offering a playful yet sophisticated introduction.

The heart reveals a floral bouquet of Turkish rose, peony, and lily of the valley, evoking
softness and charm. Finally, the base settles into vanilla, musk, and cashmeran, giving the
scent warmth and depth that lingers beautifully on the skin.

Fragrance Notes:

  • Top Notes: Lychee, Rhubarb, Bergamot
  • Heart Notes: Turkish Rose, Peony, Lily of the Valley
  • Base Notes: Vanilla, Musk, Cashmeran
